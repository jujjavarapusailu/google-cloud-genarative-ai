Epic 1: Foundational Architecture

Epic 1 (Foundational Architecture) establishes EduGenie's core structure by selecting lightweight, task-specific AI models for Question & Answer, Quiz Generation, and Content Summarization. It also designs a modular folder architecture to ensure scalable frontend, backend, and AI integration for future development.

Key Objectives
  • Select lightweight AI models for Q&A, quiz generation, and summarization.
  • Design a modular and scalable project folder structure.
  • Separate frontend, backend, and AI components for maintainability.
  • Enable seamless integration of AI services with the application.
  • Provide a strong foundation for future feature expansion.
